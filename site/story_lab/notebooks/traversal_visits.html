Traversal Visits

measured as the accumulated number of visits after traversing the network: 1. begin at every article 2. first link receives +1 visit 3. continue to next first link, +1 visit - stop at a repeated article or invalid link

    dict = json.load(f)

df.columns = ['article', 'traversal visits']

df = df.sort(columns='traversal visits', ascending=False)
In [3]:
#add a rank column and log scaled columns

df['log(traversal visits)'] = np.log(df['traversal visits']+1)
df['rank'] = np.arange(1, df.shape[0]+1)
df['log(rank)'] = np.log(df['rank'])

df['traversal visits (m)'] = df['traversal visits'] / 1000000
